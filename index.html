I’m a product designer with a strong UX/UI focus. I work across both execution and strategy. That means I can design and ship polished, responsive interfaces, but I also help teams define systems, improve workflows, and make better product decisions.

For example, I led the Design System initiative and helped unify internal and external tools at Clutch. At Booking.com, I worked on large-scale B2C and B2B products used by millions. Through Toptal, I’ve partnered with global clients on redesigns, product launches, and platform cleanups.

It always starts with context. I map out the problem space before jumping into pixels—understanding what users want to achieve, business constraints, and current friction points.

I use tools like Jobs To Be Done, “How Might We” questions, user journey mapping, or UX maturity assessments depending on the project. Then, I move quickly through flows, sketches, and prototypes, testing early and refining often. My process flexes based on the problem, the team, and the speed required.

I’m lean but intentional, balancing fast iteration with thoughtful design grounded in clear outcomes.
